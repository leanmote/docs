# Administration Overview

The Administration area is where Leanmote becomes operationally reliable for the full organization.

Your goals in this section are to:

- Connect and govern data sources through Productivity Tools.
- Keep user identities and teams mapped correctly across platforms.
- Configure settings that control visibility, access, and reporting standards.

## What strong administration unlocks

- Higher trust in every dashboard metric
- Faster onboarding for new teams and managers
- Cleaner security boundaries and audit readiness
- Fewer reporting surprises caused by mapping drift

## Core administration domains

- [Integrations](integrations.md): connection strategy, permissions, security posture, and status mapping.
- [Users and Teams Management](users-and-teams.md): user mapping, invitations, access levels, and team structures.
- [Settings](settings.md): organization-wide controls and metric visibility filters.

## Recommended operating cadence

- Weekly: review sync health and mapping issues.
- Bi-weekly: validate access levels and team ownership.
- Monthly: audit settings, scopes, and enterprise identity controls.
