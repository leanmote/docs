# Bottleneck Insights

Bottleneck Insights highlights where work slows down across your delivery lifecycle.

This view helps teams move from symptom reporting to root-cause targeting.

## Typical bottleneck categories

- Review pickup delays
- Long waiting states in workflows
- Excess WIP accumulation
- Team-specific throughput constraints

## How teams use it

1. Isolate the stage where delay concentrates.
2. Segment by team, project, or workflow type.
3. Validate whether mapping or process drift affects results.
4. Prioritize one operational fix and re-check trend movement.

## Best for

- Engineering managers running continuous improvement
- Delivery ops identifying systemic friction
- Cross-team optimization initiatives
