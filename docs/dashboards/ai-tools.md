# AI Tools

The AI Tools dashboard tracks adoption and impact of AI-assisted engineering workflows.

It helps leadership evaluate whether AI usage translates into measurable execution improvements.

## What it is for

- Monitor AI tooling adoption by team.
- Compare delivery patterns before and after adoption.
- Identify where AI contributes most to speed or quality.

## Typical questions this dashboard answers

- Which teams are getting measurable value from AI?
- Are AI-enabled teams reducing cycle or review latency?
- Where should enablement and training be focused next?

## Best for

- Engineering enablement leads
- Managers evaluating workflow modernization
- Leadership tracking AI return on investment
