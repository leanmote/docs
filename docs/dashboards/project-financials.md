# Project Financials

Project Financials connects engineering execution to investment efficiency.

Use it to understand where capacity is being spent and how output evolves by project and team.

## What you can analyze

- Delivery output relative to team effort
- Financial trend signals across projects
- Team-level contribution patterns over time
- AI and productivity related indicators (when available)

## Typical filters

- Team and project scope
- Time period
- Delivery segment or portfolio view

## Review workflow

1. Compare project-level delivery movement over time.
2. Identify cost-intensive streams with weak output trends.
3. Confirm whether bottlenecks or staffing patterns explain variance.
4. Feed decisions into strategic planning and prioritization.

## Best for

- Engineering directors
- Delivery operations
- Portfolio and planning reviews
