# Strategic Overview

Strategic Overview provides leadership with a concise, organization-level signal of engineering health.

It is optimized for directional decisions, not tactical triage.

## Primary purpose

- Align executives and engineering leadership on delivery trajectory.
- Track whether strategic initiatives improve core performance metrics.
- Support resource and priority decisions with trend evidence.

## What to monitor

- Organization-wide trend movement
- Team variance and concentration of risk
- Progress against strategic engineering goals

## Best for

- Monthly leadership business reviews
- Quarterly planning checkpoints
- Strategic investment recalibration
